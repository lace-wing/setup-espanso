【安装位置】
Windows：<Steam的安装位置>\Steam\steamapps\common\tModLoader\
Linux：~/.local/share/Steam/steamapps/common/tModLoader 或 ~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/tModLoader/
macOS：Library/Application Support/Steam/steamapps/common/tModLoader/tModLoader.app/Contents/MacOS/
你可以在 Steam 库中右键 tModLoader，选择“属性”，点击“本地文件”，“浏览……”来打开安装文件夹。
